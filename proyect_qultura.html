"La Cultura es Para Todos"

A project in partnership with the Secretary of Culture of the state of Querétaro

A web application for administrators of the Ministry of Culture of Querétaro to manage and administer a mobile application. The mobile application was aimed to promote the culture and heritage of the region. And can be found in this webpage as a separate project.

The web application allowed the administrators to upload and update information about cultural events, exhibitions, concerts, workshops, and other activities related to culture as well as update the digital galleries within the mobile app.
