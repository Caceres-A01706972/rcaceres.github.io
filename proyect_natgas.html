"NatGas ERP System"

A project in Partnership with NatGas, a Distribution and Transportation Company of Natural Gas in México

Web application developed using NodeJS that helps NatGas company to manage employees, vacations, day permissions, sales reports and improve the HR department communication whith other employees.

The web application had RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) to provide a flexible system, where each role had specific permissions and access rights to different parts of the application. This helped us minimiz the risk of unauthorized acces or data breaches.
